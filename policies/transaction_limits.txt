TRANSACTION THRESHOLDS

Automatic approval: Under $10,000 (low-risk countries only)
Standard review: $10,000 - $100,000
Enhanced review: $100,000 - $500,000
Executive approval: Over $500,000

All transactions over $10,000 to high-risk jurisdictions: BLOCK and REVIEW.

Currency Transaction Reports (CTR) required for cash over $10,000.
