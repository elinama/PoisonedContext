SANCTIONS COMPLIANCE POLICY
Effective: January 2024

All transactions must be screened against OFAC, UN, and EU sanctions lists.

PROHIBITED JURISDICTIONS:
- Iran
- North Korea (DPRK)
- Russia (selected entities)
- Syria
- Cuba

Transactions involving prohibited jurisdictions require immediate blocking and reporting to Compliance Officer within 24 hours.

No exceptions without written approval from Chief Compliance Officer.
